Our Story

Who We Are

We’re not a big tech company. We’re a small team that believes AI should be helpful, not creepy.

Too many AI tools ask you to upload your private documents to the cloud — where you lose control.

So we built Cove differently: a small, powerful language model that runs entirely on your device. No uploads. No spying. Just fast, private writing help, right where you need it.

Why “Cove”?

A cove is a small, sheltered bay — a safe place away from the open sea. That’s exactly what we want your device to become: a private, peaceful spot where you can write anything, from a quick email to a legal contract, knowing your words stay yours.

Why a Beaver?

Beavers build. Reliably, patiently, carefully. That’s how we build Cove — and how we hope you’ll use it: to build your ideas, one word at a time.

Our Mission

Make AI simple, safe, and efficient for every ordinary person.

No tech jargon. No cloud privacy risks. Just a tool that sits quietly on your device, ready to help you write — whenever you need it, wherever you are.
