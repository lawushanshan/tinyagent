Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Cove (“the App”) provides on-device AI writing assistance.

Our core promise: Your text never leaves your device. We do not send your drafts, emails, contracts, or any other content to any cloud server.

2. What data do we collect?

2.1 Data that stays on your device (we never access)

  • All text you type or generate within the App
  • Drafts, emails, contracts, and any other written content
  • Model inference results (suggestions, completions, revisions)
This data is processed locally by the AI model running on your hardware. We have no technical ability to see, store, or transfer it.

2.2 Limited data we may collect for service improvement

  • App version, device OS version, anonymized usage statistics (e.g., which features are used)
  • Crash logs (with no personal content)

You can opt out of analytics collection at any time in the App settings.

2.3 Account & payment data (if you purchase a premium plan)

  • When you sign up for a paid plan, our payment processor may collect your name, email address, and billing information.
  • We do not store full payment card details on our servers.

3. Legal basis for processing (GDPR for EU/EEA users)

  • Performance of a contract – to provide the AI writing assistance you request
  • Legitimate interest – to improve the App’s stability and features (using anonymized data only)
  • Consent – if we ever need to collect anything beyond the above, we will ask for your explicit consent first

4. Your rights under GDPR

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@cove.ai.

5. CCPA / CPRA (for California residents)

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • California residents have the right to request disclosure of the categories of personal data collected and to request deletion of their data.

6. Data transfers

Because nearly all processing happens on your device, no routine transfer of personal data occurs.

If any anonymized analytics are transferred (e.g., for crash reporting), we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and the EU-US Data Privacy Framework where applicable.

7. Children’s privacy

Cove is not intended for children under 13 (or under 16 in certain jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you via email or an in-app notice.
